\begin{conj}
	Let $C$ be a clause in a resolution refutation. 
	Suppose that $\AI\de(C)$ contains a maximal $\Gamma$-term $\gamma_j\occ{x_i}$ which contains a lifting variable $x_i$ (lifting a $\Delta$-term $\delta_i$).
	Then there is an arrow from an occurrence of $x_k$ in $\AI\de(C)$ such that $\delta_k$ is an abstraction of $\delta_i$ to $\gamma_j\occ{x_i}$.
\end{conj}
\begin{proof}
	We proceed by induction.
	For the base case, note that no multicolored terms occur in inital clauses, so no lifting term can occur inside of a $\Gamma$-term.

	Now suppose that for every lifting term inside a $\Gamma$-term in the clauses $C_1$ and $C_2$ of the refutation the desired arrows exist.
\end{proof}
